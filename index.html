Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining DevConf 2026.

📅 When is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 takes place over three days featuring keynote sessions, workshops, networking events, and hands-on labs.

🎟 What's included with my ticket?

Your ticket includes conference access, keynote sessions, technical talks, networking breaks, and exclusive event materials based on your selected pass.

💻 Do I need to bring my laptop?

Yes. Workshops and coding sessions are interactive, so bringing your own laptop is highly recommended.

🌍 Can I attend remotely?

Selected keynote sessions will be streamed online, while workshops and networking activities are available only onsite.

🤝 Will there be networking opportunities?

Absolutely. Meet engineers, founders, recruiters, and industry leaders during dedicated networking sessions throughout the conference.

📧 How will I receive my ticket?

After registration, your confirmation email and digital ticket will be sent instantly to your registered email address.
